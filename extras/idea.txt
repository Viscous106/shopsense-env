For example a shop keeper sees certain types of customers with AI:

person1: A doctor which buys medial items 80% of the time, and 20% generic things.

person2: A sports player which buys 65% times sports item, 20% times groceries and other times generic stuff.

person3: A teacher which buys 40% times stationary, 40% times groceries, 20% generic stuff.

person4: Doctor two that buys 40% times stationary, 40% times fruits, 20% generic.

A total of 10 is initially taken but the count is not fixed. data.json contains the data, to get the total count just count the total there.
 

Given the ID of the customer, the Model has to train on what he will buy.

 

The data will be fully dummy with distribution defined for each person.

Each times the ML gets it right it receives some points.

There will be a maximum score which we will determine (can be determined with simple code).

The final score has to be between 0 to 1 so we will normalize it using a normalizing funtion like normal division or better sigmoid or tanh.
